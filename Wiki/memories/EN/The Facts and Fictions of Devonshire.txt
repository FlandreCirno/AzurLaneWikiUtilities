The light is off, so only silvery moonlight illuminates it.<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
Commander, this is the final door.<br>
Once you step inside, you will be able to see the true Devonshire – one who doesn't play any character.<br>
Are you ready for this?<br>
<span style="color:#3498DB;" class="shikikanname">指挥官</span>：<br>
I was born ready.<br>
<br>
When I open the door and step inside, she giggles and locks it behind me.<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
Now, as promised... today's date enters its final phase.<br>
No script, no roles, and no pre-written plot.<br>
All I have now... are the words I want to tell you, and the self I want you to see.<br>
<br>
She slowly approaches until both of us can feel each other's breath, and she places her fingers on my collar.<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
Do you get it yet? The reason I played so many characters and contrived so many scenarios today...<br>
...Was because I wanted you to look at me alone.<br>
You and me...<br>
And...<br>
Our most authentic selves, which we can only share with each other.<br>
<br>
Pushing me up against a mirror, she braces herself with her hands on either side of me.<br>
Her fingers graze my collar again, undoing the top button of my shirt.<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
In this moment, I'm not a chef, a princess, or a fortune-teller.<br>
It's just... you and me, as we are.<br>
The Devonshire... who wants to keep you here forever.<br>
<span style="color:#3498DB;" class="shikikanname">指挥官</span>：<br>
Are you trapping me here?<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
No... I'm only expressing that I wish this moment could go on forever.<br>
<br>
She reaches for the costume rack, retrieves a length of ribbon, and ties it around our wrists.<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
Now that I've shown you my true self... I'll keep you by my side from now on.<br>
<br>
Once the ribbon is tightened enough to keep our wrists together...<br>
She raises her head so that she's face-to-face with me again.<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
You know... I hate surprises.<br>
They tend to disrupt my plans.<br>
But all of tonight's accidents have brought us closer together.<br>
That's made me change my mind. Rather than the perfect script... I might like this unexpected ending with you even more.<br>
Still, you need to be punished for disrupting my plans.<br>
<span style="color:#3498DB;" class="shikikanname">指挥官</span>：<br>
Punished?<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
From now on, you'll have to get used to the true Devonshire.<br>
I'll be selfish at times, I'll be jealous of your attention, and I'll try to go on dates with you when we have time alone... You'll need to get used to all of that.<br>
<br>
Devonshire lowers her head slightly and rests it on my shoulder.<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
Will you accept me as I am?<br>
As someone who's imperfect, inelegant, and maybe a little greedy...<br>
<br>
With my free hand, I stroke her hair.<br>
<span style="color:#3498DB;" class="shikikanname">指挥官</span>：<br>
That's the Devonshire I've been waiting to see.<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
*sigh*... I'm supposed to be the one leading this date, but it feels like you're the one caring for me.<br>
<br>
As clouds cover the moon and block off its light, the two of us lean close.<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
Promise me. From this night on, every version of me that you see – be it maid, assistant, or the true Devonshire...<br>
Whoever I may be, I want you to accept me.<br>
<span style="color:#3498DB;" class="shikikanname">指挥官</span>：<br>
I promise.<br>
<br>
Her arm circles around my waist, and she presses her knee against me to hold me in place.<br>
Warm breath touches my ear, and fingertips grasp the bottom of my shirt.<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
Your heart is racing, Commander...<br>
If I got closer... I worry you'd forget how to breathe.<br>
<br>
Her fingers trace a slow path upward.<br>
<span style="color:#4eb24e;">Devonshire：</span><br>
Tonight's punishment... is more than a little lecture.<br>
